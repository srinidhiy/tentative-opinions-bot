Case Number: 21STCV23340    Hearing Date: July 14, 2022    Dept: 3

 

SUPERIOR COURT OF CALIFORNIA

COUNTY OF LOS ANGELES – NORTHEAST DISTRICT

DEPARTMENT 3

 

 

SANDRA SANDOVAL BEN JACOB , et al.;

 

Plaintiffs,

 

 

vs.

 

 

KRISTEN ROSE UQUILLAS, M.D. , et al.,

 

Defendants.

Case No.:

21STCV23340 [c/w 21STCV33626]

 

 

Hearing Date:

July 14, 2022

 

 

Time:

8:30 a.m.

 

 

 

[TENTATIVE] ORDER RE:

 

 

PLAINTIFF CALLUM LEONARD’S MOTION FOR PREFERENTIAL TRIAL DATE

 

 

MOVING PARTY:                Plaintiff Callum Leonard

 

RESPONDING PARTY:       N/A

Plaintiff Callum Leonard’s Motion for Preferential Trial Date

The court considered the moving papers. No opposition was filed by any party. Sandra Sandoval Ben Jacob and Adam Ben Jacob, the plaintiffs in the lead case, filed a non-opposition.

 

BACKGROUND

This action was filed by Plaintiffs Sandra Sandoval Ben Jacob and Adam Ben Jacob (jointly, the “Ben Jacobs”) on June 23, 2021. A second case was filed by Plaintiff Callum Leonard, a minor, by and through his guardian ad litem, Emily Leonard (“Plaintiff”) on September 13, 2021. Both cases were ordered consolidated on March 23, 2022.

            Plaintiff now moves for an order for trial preference.

 

DISCUSSION

Code of Civil Procedure section 36, subdivision (b) provides, in pertinent part, as follows: “A civil action to recover damages for wrongful death or personal injury shall be entitled to preference upon the motion of any party to the action who is under 14 years of age unless the court finds that the party does not have a substantial interest in the case as a whole.” Upon the granting of a motion for trial preference, “the court shall set the matter for trial not more than 120 days from that date.” (Code Civ. Proc., § 36, subd. (f).)

It is undisputed that Callum Leonard is under the age of 14, and it is further undisputed that he is a plaintiff in this matter and so he has a substantial interest in the case as a whole.

CONCLUSION

Based on the foregoing, the court grants Plaintiff’s motion for trial preference.

Trial shall be on ____, 2023, at 8:30 a.m. in Dept. 3. The final status conference shall be on _____, 2023, at 9:00 a.m. in Dept. 3.  

Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of this ruling.

IT IS SO ORDERED.

 

DATED:  July 14, 2022
